## Health & Safety & Environmental Risk Coverage (IOSH-aligned)
The register includes dedicated Health & Safety and environmental risks relevant to field-based operations, including:

- Adverse weather during outdoor work (rain, wind, heat, cold)
- Slips, trips, and falls on location
- Manual handling injuries (equipment transport and setup)
- Heat stress and cold exposure
- Vehicle and journey-related safety risks

Controls reflect practical H&S expectations, such as:
- Site-specific risk assessments
- Stop-work authority and weather thresholds
- Training and safe systems of work
- PPE considerations
- Journey planning and fatigue management
- Review and monitoring of incidents and near misses
